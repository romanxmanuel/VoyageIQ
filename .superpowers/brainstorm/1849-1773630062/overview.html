What's working, what's not — VoyageIQ audit

Here's everything I found after reading the full codebase. Pick which problems to solve first.

Problems found
A

Search only works for 9 destinations

Type "Bangkok" or "Rome" and it silently falls back to Tokyo. No autocomplete. No error message. User has no idea why the result looks wrong.

B

Philippines is limited to 4 islands

Boracay, El Nido, Bohol, Siargao only. No Manila, Sorsogon, Naga, Cebu, Davao, or any Luzon destinations at all.

C

Form language is too technical / jargon-heavy

"Lean Explorer", "Elevated Flow", "Signature Memory", "Match confidence: Seeded spotlight", "Awaiting query" — none of this means anything to a regular traveler.

D

No "family trip" mode for older parents

No walkability filter, no "easy travel" preference, no flight connection limits, no medical/comfort indicators. Hard to plan for someone with mobility needs.

E

Other UX friction

No origin airport autocomplete. "Nights" vs "Days" is confusing. No budget input (can't say "I have $5,000 to spend"). No trip type selector (beach, city, nature).
